About the Mela

Join us for the most awaited cultural extravaganza of the year! The Makar Sankranti Mela 2026, hosted by Ganga Bhumi Club, celebrates the rich heritage and vibrant traditions of our land. Get ready for a day filled with exciting competitions, delicious food, mesmerizing performances, and the joyous spirit of the kite festival.

This event brings our community together to honor our roots. Don't miss out on this unforgettable celebration!
